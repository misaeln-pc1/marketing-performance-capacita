# Master Prompts para IA Generativa - Capacita.cl

## 1. Reglas de Marca (Brand Guidelines)
* **Estilo Visual:** Realismo fotográfico corporativo, iluminación natural, colores sobrios.
* **Elementos Obligatorios:** Notebooks encendidos en las mesas, manuales impresos visibles, actitud de concentración y alivio.
* **Exclusiones:** Evitar estilos cartoon, luces de neón, elementos futuristas o salas vacías.

## 2. Prompt para "Desbordado Operativo" (Campaña Mayo)
**Prompt (Optimizado para motores de imagen):**
> "Photorealistic medium shot of a professional adult in their 30s sitting at a modern desk in a brightly lit training room. They are looking at a laptop screen showing an Excel spreadsheet, smiling with a sense of relief and control. On the desk, there is a printed training manual and a cup of coffee. The background shows a clean, air-conditioned classroom setting in a corporate building. High quality, 8k resolution, cinematic lighting."

## 3. Prompt para "Reinserción Laboral" (Campaña Mayo)
**Prompt (Optimizado para motores de imagen):**
> "Photorealistic wide shot of a small, focused classroom with maximum 20 adults. In the foreground, a 40-year-old student is shaking hands with an instructor, receiving a course certificate. The student looks proud, employable, and confident. Every desk has an open laptop. Corporate training environment, natural daylight, highly detailed, professional photography."
